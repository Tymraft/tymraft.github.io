# Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Tymraft team! This guide will help you get set up.

## Step 1: Join the Claude Team

Accept your invite to the Tymraft Claude Teams workspace.

## Step 2: Install the Plugin

In the Tymraft project, say to Claude:

> "Read my getting started guide at https://tymraft.github.io/GETTING_STARTED.md and set me up."

## Step 3: You're ready!

Claude will confirm once your environment is configured.
